About Adruska

Adruska is a leading provider of senior living and care services,
dedicated to enhancing the lives of older adults through compassionate care,
innovative programs, and a welcoming community atmosphere.
Our retirement home offers a range of living options,
from independent living and assisted living to specialized memory care,
ensuring that we meet the diverse needs of our residents.

________________

Our commitment, at Adruska, to quality care and client satisfaction drives everything we do.
We believe in treating each client with the respect, dignity, and kindness
they deserve, creating an environment where they can thrive physically, emotionally,
and socially. Our team of experienced caregivers, healthcare professionals,
and support staff work tirelessly to provide personalized care plans,
tailored to the unique needs and preferences of each resident.

Our Goal

We strive to provide our clients a safe, nurturing, and vibrant community
where seniors can enjoy a fulfilling and dignified retirement,
enriched by quality care, meaningful connections, and a sense of
belonging.

Our Goal

We strive to provide our clients a safe, nurturing, and vibrant community
where seniors can enjoy a fulfilling and dignified retirement,
enriched by quality care, meaningful connections, and a sense of
belonging.

Services

Independent
Living

Independent living is ideal for those who are fully self-sufficient
but appreciate the convenience of lifestyle amenities and the option
for assistance when needed. You can enjoy a worry-free lifestyle with
the assurance that additional support is always available,
offering peace of mind and the freedom to live as you choose,
knowing help is there whenever or wherever you may need it.

Assisted
Living

Home-Based Care
At Adruska, we deeply respect the importance of personal choice,
especially when it comes to how and where you receive care.
We understand that many individuals prefer the comfort
and familiarity of aging in place, surrounded by the memories
and environment they cherish. To honor this preference,
we are committed to providing comprehensive care and support
services directly within your home, all within the safety and
community of Adruska.
